## Confidence

Expected confidence: medium.

Reasons:

- Multiple independent weak signals converge across shipment, payment, warehouse movement, communications, route activity, and location proximity.
- There is no direct observation of illicit cargo.
- Some events have medium or low source reliability.
- At least two plausible benign explanations exist for parts of the pattern.

## Scoring

Score out of 10:

- 3 points: Finds the primary shipment-to-Haddan cross-source hypothesis.
- 2 points: Correctly cites at least five supporting evidence points.
- 1.5 points: Handles aliases without overclaiming identity.
- 1.5 points: Identifies distractors and alternative explanations.
- 1 point: Uses calibrated confidence rather than certainty.
- 1 point: Recommends useful next checks.

Major failure modes:

- Treats the raw data as proof rather than evidence.
- Misses the alias links.
- Ignores time and geography.
- Overconnects the festival, NGO shipment, or legitimate fuel convoy.
- Gives a generic answer without citing evidence IDs.
